Our Facility Info:

Our Goal and Passion

The goal of our facility is to provide a safe and secure environment for all inmates. We strive to ensure that all inmates are treated with respect and dignity. Our facility is designed to rehabilitate inmates and help them reintegrate into society. We believe that everyone deserves a second chance and we are committed to helping inmates achieve their goals.

Facility Levels

Level 1:
This level is designed for the most dangerous and violent offenders. They will be given less freedom then the lower levels and will be kept in a high security dorm. These dorms include a 24/7 guardian that will oversee all the inmates. Inmates will be given 4 hours of free time a day that is seperate to the other to levels.

Level 2:
This level is designed for less dangerous offenders. They will be given more freedom then the level 1 inmates and will be kept in a medium security dorm. These dorms include a 12/7 guardian that will oversee all the inmates. Inmates will be given 8 hours of free time a day that is seperate to the other to levels. These inmates will still need to request there time for activites and will be given a time limit depending on the activity.

Level 3:
This level is designed for the least dangerous offenders. They will be given the most freedom and will be kept in a low security dorm. These dorms include a 6/7 guardian that will oversee all the inmates. Inmates will be given 12 hours of free time a day that is seperate to the other to levels. These inmates will not need to request there time for activites and will be given a time limit depending on the activity.
