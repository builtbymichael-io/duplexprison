Regulations

The following regulations are in place to ensure the safety and security of all inmates and staff:

General Regulations

  • All inmates must follow the rules and regulations of the facility.
  • Inmates must respect the staff and other inmates at all times.
  • Inmates are not allowed to possess any contraband items.
  • Inmates must attend all scheduled activities and programs.
  • Inmates must follow the dress code at all times.
  • Inmates must not engage in any violent or disruptive behavior.
  • Inmates must not use any electronic devices without permission.

Visitation Regulations

  • Inmates are allowed to have visitors during scheduled visitation hours.
  • Visitors must be approved by the facility before visiting.
  • Visitors must follow the dress code and conduct regulations.
  • Visitors are not allowed to bring any contraband items into the facility.
  • Visitors must sign in and out at the front desk.

Contraband Regulations

  • Contraband items are not allowed in the facility.
  • Contraband items include weapons, drugs, and any other illegal items.
  • Inmates found with contraband items will face disciplinary action.
  • Visitors found with contraband items will be banned from visiting the facility.

Disciplinary Action

Inmates who violate the regulations will face disciplinary action, which may include:

  • Loss of privileges
  • Isolation
  • Transfer to a higher security level
  • Extended sentence
  • Change in rehabilitation program

Conclusion

The regulations in place at the Duplex Corrections Facility are designed to ensure the safety and security of all inmates and staff. It is important for all inmates to follow these regulations to maintain a safe and secure environment.

Our Facility

The Duplex Corrections Facility is a state-of-the-art prison designed to provide a safe and secure environment for all inmates. We focus on rehabilitation and reintegration into society. Our facility is equipped with the latest security technology and is staffed by trained professionals who are dedicated to ensuring the safety and well-being of all inmates.

Below are some of the key features of our facility:

  • High security dorms for dangerous offenders
  • Medium security dorms for less dangerous offenders
  • Low security dorms for least dangerous offenders
  • Rehabilitation programs to help inmates reintegrate into society
  • Life skills training to help inmates develop the skills they need to succeed
  • Security rooms with 24/7 surveillance to ensure the safety of all inmates
  • Dining hall for all inmates to enjoy meals together

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe and secure environment for all inmates while also focusing on rehabilitation and reintegration into society. We believe that everyone deserves a second chance and we are committed to helping inmates achieve their goals. Our facility is designed to help inmates develop the skills they need to succeed and to provide them with the support they need to reintegrate into society.

Current Stats

As of today, the Duplex Corrections Facility has a total of 500 inmates across all levels. The breakdown of inmates by level is as follows:

  • Level 1: 150 inmates
  • Level 2: 200 inmates
  • Level 3: 150 inmates

Our facility is currently operating at 80% capacity, with room for additional inmates if needed. We are committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all inmates and will continue to work towards our mission of rehabilitation and reintegration into society.

Visitation

Visitation is an important part of the rehabilitation process for inmates. It allows inmates to maintain connections with their families and friends, which can help them reintegrate into society after their release. Our facility has a strict visitation policy to ensure the safety and security of all inmates and visitors.

Visitation Hours

Visitation hours are as follows:

  • Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • Saturday and Sunday: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM

Visitors must arrive at least 30 minutes before the scheduled visitation time to allow for security checks. All visitors must be approved by the facility before visiting.

Visitation Rules

The following rules must be followed during visitation:

  • Visitors must sign in and out at the front desk.
  • Visitors must follow the dress code and conduct regulations.
  • Visitors are not allowed to bring any contraband items into the facility.
  • Visitors must not engage in any violent or disruptive behavior.
  • Visitors must respect the staff and other visitors at all times.
  • Visitors must not take any photographs or videos during visitation.
  • Visitors must not use any electronic devices during visitation.

Failure to follow these rules may result in the termination of visitation privileges. We take the safety and security of all inmates and visitors very seriously and will not tolerate any violations of the visitation rules.
